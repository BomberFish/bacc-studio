How to use üÖ±Ô∏èacc Studio

üÖ±Ô∏èacc Studio is designed for simplicity.
Drag your clips onto the window to add them to the content browser
(on the top-left), then drag them onto the timeline (on the bottom).

You can use the player (top-right) to play through your project
while you're working on it.

You can save your progress by clicking on File > Save, and open it
again by clicking File > Open and selecting the .bacc file you saved earlier.

Once you've finished your video, click File > Export to save your video as a video
file and upload it to sites like YouTube.
